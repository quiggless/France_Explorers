Discover all that Alsace has to offer!

What is there to do in Alsace?

  • Explore Strasbourg Cathedral

    Embark on a fascinating journey through history as you explore the awe-inspiring Strasbourg Cathedral. This masterpiece of Gothic architecture stands as a testament to centuries of craftsmanship and religious significance. Admire the intricate details of its facade, including stunning sculptures and delicate carvings. Don't miss the opportunity to climb to the top for panoramic views of the charming city below. With its UNESCO World Heritage status, Strasbourg Cathedral is not just a religious site but a cultural and architectural gem that captivates visitors with its grandeur and historical significance.

  • Wine Tasting in Riquewihr

    Indulge your senses in the enchanting village of Riquewihr, where the art of winemaking comes to life. Enjoy a delightful wine-tasting experience amidst the rolling vineyards of Alsace. Sample a variety of exquisite Alsatian wines, each with its unique flavor profile. Engage with knowledgeable vintners who share insights into the region's rich winemaking traditions. The charming surroundings of Riquewihr provide the perfect backdrop for a leisurely exploration of the vineyards, allowing you to appreciate not only the taste of the wines but also the beauty of the landscape and the heritage of the Alsatian wine culture.

  • Visit Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg

    Transport yourself to the medieval era with a visit to the majestic Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg. Perched atop a picturesque hill, this well-preserved castle offers a captivating journey through time. Immerse yourself in the rich history of the region as you explore the castle's impressive architecture, including towers, ramparts, and interior rooms. The panoramic views from the castle provide a breathtaking glimpse of the Alsace region. Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg is not only a symbol of medieval splendor but also a place where visitors can connect with the past and appreciate the strategic importance of this historic fortress.

  • Wander the Streets of Colmar

    Step into a fairy-tale setting as you wander through the charming streets of Colmar. This Alsatian town is a visual delight, adorned with colorful half-timbered houses, flower-filled canals, and cobblestone pathways. Immerse yourself in the unique charm of Colmar, exploring local markets, boutique shops, and historic landmarks. Take leisurely strolls along the canals, where the reflection of charming buildings creates a picturesque scene. Colmar's inviting atmosphere extends to its cozy cafes and restaurants, offering the perfect opportunity to savor the renowned Alsatian cuisine while surrounded by the town's enchanting ambiance.

  • Hike the Vosges Mountains

    Escape into the natural beauty of Alsace by embarking on a rejuvenating hike through the Vosges Mountains. Choose from a variety of scenic trails that lead you through lush forests, serene landscapes, and panoramic viewpoints. The Vosges Mountains offer not only a physical adventure but also a chance to connect with nature and appreciate the region's diverse flora and fauna. Whether you're an avid hiker seeking challenging paths or a casual walker enjoying a leisurely stroll, the Vosges Mountains provide an immersive experience in the heart of Alsace's breathtaking and unspoiled natural surroundings.
